Why I Write

Anna Liu

CS & Bio Student at Duke

Hey there! I see that you have stumbled upon my Thoughts and Musings. Allow me to introduce myself.

My name is Anna. I’m currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Biology at Duke. I’m an avid programmer, tech junkie, wannabe biologist. In the past two years, I have probably spent more time coding in front of a computer or conducting research in a laboratory than actually writing in English.

But I do love to write. And I am thankful, and often relieved, for those rare opportunities where I can express my thoughts in English prose.

Writing keeps me sane. It helps me to collect my thoughts and contemplate about the life that I am living beyond a computer screen or a lab bench.

Writing stimulates my curiosity. It helps me to identify research interests, explore topics of interest, and formulate new questions.

Writing reminds me that I am human. It helps me to reflect upon my social experiences and learn from my mistakes.
