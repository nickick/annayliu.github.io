Think and Muse

Anna Liu

Graduated from Duke. Moved to Silicon Valley. Forever a blue devil.

For the past three months, I have been actively publishing posts on this blog Thoughts and Musings. Whether I write about tech, product, and ideas that spark my mind, I always strive to jot down the reflections of my spontaneous mind.

My thoughts and musings are ephemeral, but I believe that creative, analytical, and insightful writing will ensure their longevity. I try to make my writings intellectually engaging, and if my posts have inspired your own thoughts and musings, then this blog has achieved its purpose.

Thought and Musings was originally created on my college's WordPress platform as a writing portfolio to collect what I have learned as a liberal arts student at Duke University. Now, it has been transformed into a creative outlet, a digital corner for my mind to grow and ponder.

People say that learning is a lifelong passion. Thoughts and Musings is the output of my attempt to make learning my lifelong passion.

I am always curious. I think. I muse.

I also write on Medium.

P.S.

If you ever get a chance, visit the Sarah P. Duke Gardens at Duke University in the spring. The place is a real treat come April and May. It was one of the reasons that I chose to attend Duke. It has been my zen, a transcendental escape from the hustle and bustle of undergraduate student life.
