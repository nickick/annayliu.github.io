Hack the Mind By Loving

Anna Liu

Graduated from Duke. Moved to Silicon Valley. Forever a blue devil.

Hack the mind by living, laughing, learning, and loving.

Loving.

Love is expressed through actions, and experienced through emotions. However, there is no one way to define the essence of love.

Love encompasses sympathy, determination, tolerance, patience, support, confidence and so much more. If a person wants to truly express love, he or she must first learn to love oneself, to love the world, and to love mankind.

If a person wishes for more love in their lives, the person must learn to accept the past. You must not let past things hold you back from finding true love and happiness. Once you have accepted the past, forgive and forget, and move on.

When finding love, one must be open-minded and consider the qualities of a person that does not initially meet the eye. One must remember that no one is perfect, and never close the door on a new opportunity.

Take a fresh look at all the people you encounter in your daily life, and you might discover someone as a love potential. At first, potential may not seem as exciting as love at first sight. However, give this potential time, and it might just blossom into the most stable and beautiful love you have ever experienced.

To love others, you must first learn to love yourself. You should learn how to experience love, build self-confidence, and change your bad habits. Do something good for someone else so that you can become a better person. To respect yourself is to love yourself. Remember that no one in this world perfect. However, if you learn to love yourself, people will notice the change through your behavior and interaction with them. Once you love yourself, then you are worth being loved by others.

Once you begin a relationship, you must learn how to contribute to the relationship because love is shared between two people. Love has its ups and downs. A lover will bring you joy and happiness, but he or she can also hurt you.

If you want true love, you must learn to open your heart and trust your loved one. Do not keep secrets from each other. Do not pretend what you are not. Do not lie to each other. Give your lover the chance to know the real you.

Lastly, you need to give love time. Do not force the feeling of love. Do not hastily rush into relationship. This will only leave you feeling discontent and empty in the relationship. Love takes patience and endurance.

Love is waiting. You only need to find it.

P.S.

I know that what I wrote above may be sappy, cliched, or completely unrealistic. But, it isÂ my perception on love.

Having attended my very first wedding this past March, I know that happily ever after is not a fairy tale.

Love takes time, effort, money, and energy. But I believe that if you put everything into a relationship with someone who both your gut, mind, and heart tells you to go after, what do you have to lose on love?
