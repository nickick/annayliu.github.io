Hack the Mind By Laughing

Anna Liu

Graduated from Duke. Moved to Silicon Valley. Forever a blue devil.

Hack your mind by living, laughing, learning, and loving.

Laughing.

LOL (laughing out loud) is the three letter acronym that has been incorporated into my vernacular.

Laughing is the best way to convey your happiness and to release stress. It will help you lift the burden from your shoulders and free you from your worries.

Reseach on laughter has shown that laughter can help keep our bodies strong and disease resistant. A good laugh exercises many muscles in your face and causes the brain to release endorphins, which will elevate your mood and make you happy.

Simply put, laughing improves your longevity, thus giving you even more chances to pursue the life that you have always envisioned. The positive side effects of laughter include reduced blood pressure, decreased blood glucose, the dulling of pain, the alleviation of stress and anxiety, and an improved feeling of well being.

Listen up, especially for you fitness fanatics out there, laughing will help you burn those calories. A healthy laugh session can be equal to a couple of minutes on an exercising machine. In addition, when you laugh, you are also conditioning your diaphragm, abdominals, respiratory, facial, leg and back muscles. Now that's a workout you can do without a drop of sweat!

Laughing can help you feel and look better. So why not turn that frown upside down, and laugh out loud?

And if you want a laughing challenge, try ROFL (rolling on floor laughing) and LMFAO (laughing my #$@! beep off).
