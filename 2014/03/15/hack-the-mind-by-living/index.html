Hack the Mind By Living

Anna Liu

Graduated from Duke. Moved to Silicon Valley. Forever a blue devil.

Hack your mind by living, laughing, learning, and loving.

Living.

Honestly, live! You only live once (#yolo), so why not live life to the fullest?

Don't let your inhibitions and doubts hope you back from fulfilling your dreams and reaching your potential. From personal experience, I can affirm that it is so much better to live a life of "oh wells" than "what ifs."

Living is the reason that I decided to jump off a plane and go skydiving. Living is the reason that despite facing a huge mountain of debt, I still plan on a month long trip to Europe after college graduation.

I know I probably just threw a lot of mundane, cliched phrases at you, but think again. If people say these things over and over again, there must be some truth to them, right?

You only have one life to live, so live life to the fullest. Chase your dreams, set high ambitions, and never doubt yourself.

I expect to face far greater social challenges than technical ones as I prepare to enter the tech industry. But I have come to realize that the greatest obstacle to accomplishing your goals is having a negative mindset. I need to be assertive about what I want in my life.

I should not wait for things in my life to happen. I should make them happen.

Eleanor Roosevelt once said,

No one can make you feel inferior without your consent.

I try my best to not worry about what others think about me. My time is better well spent living my own life and spending my time with the friends and family that truly care about me.

As long as you set your mind to live the impossible, then you can live the impossible. And if you find that others are an impediment on your path of success, try to navigate around them with a sense of humor.

Life has its ups and downs, and it may not always be like how you envisioned. But, push yourself to make your life how you want it to be.

Live like you're shooting for the moon. And even if you miss, you will still land among the stars!
